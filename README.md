# MakeMeLaugh_GGJ24

_Get ready to split your sides at the Laugh Hole, the most uproarious comedy club this side of Montreal! Join forces with your pals in a Telestrations-style joke-off, where the only currency is laughter and the richest person is the one who can make a room roar. Witness the comedic spectacle as your collective wit is brought to life by our state-of-the-art AI comedian. It's not just virtual, it's virtually hilarious!_


## Technology Notes
* Used PolyCam to make a model with photogrammetry
* Used YamNet model to detect laughter from audio
* Used Unity Engine to build the game
